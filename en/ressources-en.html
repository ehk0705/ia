Resources & References

Resources to go further in AI and generative AI

A curated selection of books, links, online courses and practical tools to deepen the topics covered in the course: AI, deep learning, generative models, ethics.

Recommended books

  • Deep Learning – Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, Aaron Courville.
  • Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach – Stuart Russell, Peter Norvig.
  • Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow – Aurélien Géron (O’Reilly).
  • Grokking Deep Learning – Andrew Trask.
  • Machine Learning Yearning – Andrew Ng (available online for free).

Key papers and articles

  • Attention Is All You Need – the original Transformer paper.
  • Original GAN papers (Goodfellow et al.).
  • Overview papers for GPT, LLaMA, Gemini and other large models.
  • Historical work on Rosenblatt’s perceptron.

Online courses & videos

  • DeepLearning.AI courses (Andrew Ng) on AI and generative AI.
  • MIT OpenCourseWare for machine learning and deep learning.

Platforms and tools

  • Online notebooks platforms (Jupyter, Google Colab, etc.).
  • Python libraries: PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras, scikit-learn.
